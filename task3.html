William Blake (28 November 1757 - 12 August 1827) was an english poet,painter,and printmarker.Largely unrecognized during his life,Blake is now considered the history of the poetry and visual art of the Romantic age.

Although Blake was considered mad by contemporaries for his idiosyncratic views,he is held in high regard by later critics for his expressiveness and creativity.
